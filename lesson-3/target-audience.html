Audience

This website is meant for people of the age group 18 and older. It is desined to help those who have small or big businesses network and join a community of people with the same interests. These people will be able to do three things and that is to connect, build and promote each other. A safe place for those who are starting a business to see what others are doing and how they can improve too. For those experienced they can introduce othersw to the business field and build them.

Personas

First Persona

Meet Jake, middle aged man who owns a small tech business. He recently quit his job and decided to do his business full-time and spend time with family. He wishes to connect with others and build his network. he is also looking for people who could team up with him in this business.

Second Persona

Meet a small group of friends who decided after college they would like to start a business together. They had a great time working together in a group at school and enjoy each other's company. They have little or knowledge about the business industry and are looking forward to build their business from the ground up and their network too.
