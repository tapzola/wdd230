White Space

Apple

www.apple.com

In this example, not only does Apple execute active whitespace throughout the website well by placing imagery strategically to draw the users eye to specific elements down the page, but the company also uses passive whitespace to guide the user through the content without a hitch.

PARC: Alignment

AbbotsFordConvent

www.abbotsfordconvent.com

On the homepage, just about every single section is left-aligned, as are many other of their inner pages. When you visit a page, navigation is on the left and the content on the right. This is a perfect way of showing how this website is aligned in a good way.

PARC: Contrast

Panic

panicstudio.tv

You can see that this website is very colorful but all these colors blend in together with the right contrast. The words also come out alive in this website because they are blended well.
